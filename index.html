Veg Biryani Recipe

Follow this easy recipe to make flavorful vegetable biryani with aromatic spices and tender rice.

Ingredients

Rice

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 4 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Vegetables & Spices

  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potato)
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Boil water with spices and salt, add the rice, and cook until it is partially done.
  3. Heat oil or ghee, sauté onions until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and tomato.
  4. Add vegetables, spices, and salt. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Layer the rice over the vegetables, cover, and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
Tip: Rinse the rice well and let it rest after cooking for the best texture.
